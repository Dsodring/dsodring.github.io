About me

I graduated from a Higher Technical Examination (HTX) in Roskilde, Denmark in June 2017, focusing on the subjects Communication/IT, Design and English.

In order to follow my ambitions of working in the tech industry I made a decision to take a full Bachelor’s Degree abroad in Manchester, UK. I am currently in my third year at Manchester Metropolitan University, studying Digital Media and Communications.

I always try to push myself and learn new things and moving abroad at a younger age left me no choice but to be more mature and take full responsibility for my own actions and it has been an amazing journey with a lot of learning experiences along the way.

In my free time I like to read up on the latest news and trends in the web and UX design industry or improve my design and coding skills by taking on personal projects which you can find on my portfolio danielsodring.com.
