UX Design - University Project

Purpose of This Project

This project was part of my final year at Manchester Metropolitan University. The outcome of this project was a report and a high fidelity mockup of a product.

This project allowed us to use and learn a range of tools and techniques employed by User Experience practitioners to research, design, prototype, test and evaluate a digital technology. These processes were completed in teams.

Objective & Approach

The objective of this project was to follow a typical UX process and create a digital product, with an accompanying report.

As this was a project with a hard deadline, a simplified UX process was used throughout this project to ensure the team could meet the deadlines.

The process and deliverables produced in this projects for each stage were the following:

  • Research/Ideation
    • Personas
    • User Journey
    • Storyboards
    • Competitor Analysis
  • Design
    • Sitemap
    • Sketches & Wireframes
    • High fidelity prototype
  • Test
    • Usability test on high fidelity prototype
  • Iterate

Project Duration

September 16th, 2019 - December 13th, 2019.

My Role

My role within this team was researcher and designer, I was in charge of creating and designing the app in Figma and conducting usability tests with the remaining team members taking notes.
